FURNACE DETAIL

1.HEARTH

It is a receptacle to collect liquid slag and metal and is also refered to as a crucible . Made up of CARBON BLOCKS. Water cooled copper or steel plates in side walls to protect the lining. Tap hole for iron 0.3- 0.6 m above hearth bottom. Slag notch 1.2-1.6 m above iron tap hole.Closed with clay when not in use. At top level of hearth are tuyeres located uniformly.

2.BOSH

Top of the bosh has maximum diameter and is zone of intense heat. It is stadium type construction with steel reinforcement. water cooled copper plates inserted at regular intervals at furnace lining , to protect against hingh temperature.The intensive cooling of thin walled bosh forms a layer of solidified slag mixed graphite on the lining which in reality protect the inner surfaxe of lining.
